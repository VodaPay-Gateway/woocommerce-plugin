This is the official VodaPay Digital Payment Gateway extension to receive payments for WooCommerce.

== Description ==

The VodaPay Digital Payment Gateway extension for WooCommerce enables you to accept payments via the VodaPay Digital Payment Gateway payment gateway.

= Why choose VodaPay Digital Payment Gateway? =

We provide a secure checkout experience for your shoppers, knowing that intelligent fraud protection engines monitor your transactions around the clock.
